Our Story

Founded in 2010, Sinnamon Restaurant has been a beacon of fine dining, offering a luxurious experience that blends exquisite flavors with warm hospitality. Our chefs craft each dish with passion, ensuring that every bite tells a story of culinary excellence and innovation.

Our Mission

To provide our guests with a dining experience that delights the senses, combines quality ingredients, and celebrates the art of cuisine. We strive to create a welcoming atmosphere where tradition meets modernity.

Our Values

  • Quality: We source only the freshest and finest ingredients.
  • Hospitality: We treat each guest as part of the Sinnamon family.
  • Innovation: We constantly explore new flavors and techniques.

Meet Our Head Chef

Our head chef Aakil , brings a wealth of experience from renowned kitchens around the world. With a deep respect for culinary traditions and a flair for innovation, Aakil leads our team in creating dishes that leave lasting impressions.
